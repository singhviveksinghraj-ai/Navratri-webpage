Navratri is a vibrant Hindu festival celebrated for nine nights, honoring the goddess Durga in her nine forms. It usually happens in autumn and is full of colors, music, and dance, especially the traditional Garba and Dandiya dances. People fast, decorate their homes, and wear bright clothes to celebrate the victory of good over evil. What makes Navratri interesting is that each day is dedicated to a different form of the goddess, and communities come together to celebrate, making it both a spiritual and joyful festival.

Navratri is celebrated nine days
